Our Story

The Match Point Project is a student-led initiative dedicated to making tennis accessible to every kid. Our mission is to give back to our community by collecting spare tennis gear and money.

We strive to ensure the next generation will continue to be able to participate in tennis at the highest level. We aim to ensure no child is left on the sidelines due to the cost of equipment.

How You Can Join the Initiative

Donate Gear

Have old racquets, unused tennis balls, or extra gear lying around? We'll clean them up and get them into the hands of eager young players.

Financial Support

Every dollar raised goes directly toward funding court time, coaching clinics, and brand new equipment for kids in need.

Spread the Word

As a student-led team, awareness is everything. Share our mission with your local high schools, tennis clubs, and communities.
